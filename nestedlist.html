Recipe of simple sponge cake

  1. Indgredients
    • 3 eggs
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 85g self-raising flour
  2. Notes on indgredients
    • Finely ranulated caster white sugar.
    • A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agent.(usually salt and baking powder)
  3. Method
    • Preheat the oven to 190 degre.e
    • grease a 20 cm round cake pan.
    • In a medium bowl whip together the eggs and caaster sugar until fluffy.
    • fold in flour.
    • pour mixture into the prepared pan.
    • Bake for 20 minutes.
    • Cook in the an over a wie rack.
